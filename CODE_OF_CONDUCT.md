# AcreetionOS Community Guidelines

## Our Philosophy

AcreetionOS is a software project. We are here to build.

We welcome contributors based on their **ability to do what they say they will do** — not based on their identity, background, or beliefs.

## Guidelines

- **Your background, identity, politics, religion, sexuality, etc. are yours alone.** We do not require you to disclose or hide anything. Bring your best code and leave the rest at the door.

- **We do not police speech.** This is not a safe space from ideas — it is a space for building software. Disagreement and debate are part of engineering.

- **We judge code, not people.** A bad patch is a bad patch regardless of who wrote it. A good patch is a good patch regardless of who wrote it.

- **Be excellent to each other.** We won't create rules for every possible interaction. Treat people with basic respect and assume good faith.

- **Harassment of maintainers or users will not be tolerated.** This is an **action-oriented** standard, not a thought-policing regime. If you are actively harassing someone — stalking, threatening, doxxing, or similar — you will be removed.

- **This is a software project.** We measure success by working code, stable releases, and happy users — not by how well we perform ideological signaling.

## Enforcement

Maintainers have final say on community membership. If someone is actively disrupting the project or harassing others, they will be asked to leave. We don't maintain a list of forbidden words or thoughtcrimes — we deal with real problems as they arise.

## The Goal

Build great software. Help people. Move forward.
